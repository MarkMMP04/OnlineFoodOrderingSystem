Chicken Adobo

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken pieces
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 5 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 bay leaves

Procedure:

  1. Marinate chicken in soy sauce, vinegar, and garlic for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the chicken until tender and sauce thickens.

Sinigang na Baboy

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork ribs
  • 1 pack tamarind mix
  • Tomatoes, onion, green chili
  • Kangkong, radish, eggplant, okra

Procedure:

  1. Boil pork until tender.
  2. Add vegetables and tamarind mix; simmer until cooked.

Kare-Kare

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg oxtail
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup toasted rice flour
  • Vegetables (eggplant, string beans, banana blossom)

Procedure:

  1. Boil oxtail until tender.
  2. Stir in peanut butter and rice flour to thicken.
  3. Add vegetables, then serve with bagoong.

Laing

Ingredients:

  • Dried taro leaves
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • Shrimp paste or dried fish
  • Chili, onion, garlic

Procedure:

  1. Layer taro leaves in coconut milk mixture.
  2. Simmer until leaves are soft and sauce thickens.

Pancit Canton

Ingredients:

  • 250g pancit canton noodles
  • 100g pork, sliced thinly
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 1 cup cabbage, shredded
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 cup chicken broth

Procedure:

  1. Saut√© garlic and onion, then add pork until lightly browned.
  2. Pour in soy sauce, oyster sauce, and chicken broth; simmer for a few minutes.
  3. Add noodles and toss until fully cooked and liquid is absorbed.
  4. Mix in vegetables, cook briefly, and serve hot.

Lumpiang Shanghai

Ingredients:

  • 500g ground pork
  • 1 small carrot, finely grated
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 25 lumpia wrappers
  • Cooking oil for frying

Procedure:

  1. In a bowl, combine pork, carrot, onion, garlic, egg, soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  2. Wrap about 1 tablespoon of the mixture in each lumpia wrapper and seal the edges with water.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry lumpia until golden brown and crispy.
  4. Drain excess oil and serve hot with sweet chili sauce.

Lechon

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole pork belly or whole pig (for traditional lechon)
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1 head garlic, crushed
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 stalks lemongrass
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Banana leaves (for stuffing and wrapping)

Procedure:

  1. Clean the pork thoroughly and pat dry.
  2. Rub the inside with salt, pepper, soy sauce, garlic, onion, and lemongrass.
  3. Stuff the cavity with banana leaves and secure it tightly.
  4. Roast over charcoal for 3‚Äì4 hours, turning occasionally until the skin is golden and crispy.
  5. Let it rest before chopping and serve with liver sauce or vinegar dip.

Bibingka

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice flour
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • Banana leaves
  • Salted egg slices and grated cheese (for topping)

Procedure:

  1. Preheat oven to 190¬∞C (375¬∞F) and line molds with banana leaves.

  3. Add coconut milk, eggs, and melted butter. Mix until smooth.
  4. Pour batter into the lined molds and top with salted egg and cheese.
  5. Bake for 20‚Äì25 minutes until golden brown. Brush with butter before serving.

Turon

Ingredients:

  • 6 ripe saba bananas (halved lengthwise)
  • 1 cup ripe jackfruit strips (langka)
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 10 lumpia wrappers
  • Cooking oil for frying

Procedure:

  1. Roll each banana slice in brown sugar until coated.
  2. Place a banana slice and jackfruit strip on a lumpia wrapper and roll tightly, sealing with water.
  3. Heat oil in a pan and fry turon until golden brown.
  4. Sprinkle a bit of sugar into the pan to caramelize and coat the turon.
  5. Drain excess oil and serve warm.

Halo-Halo

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup crushed ice
  • 1/4 cup sweetened banana (saba)
  • 1/4 cup sweetened red beans
  • 1/4 cup nata de coco
  • 1/4 cup kaong (sugar palm fruit)

  • 1/4 cup sweetened jackfruit (langka)
  • Evaporated milk (to taste)

Procedure:

  2. Add crushed ice on top until the glass is full.
  3. Pour evaporated milk over the ice.
  4. Top with a scoop of ube ice cream and a slice of leche flan.
